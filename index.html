How to Play Pétanque
The essentials for new players
01
The Objective
Get your boules (metal balls) as close as possible to the cochonnet — a small wooden target ball also called the "jack" or "piglet".
Closest boule to the cochonnet wins the round
02
Teams & Boules
Play 1v1 (singles): 3 boules each. Play 2v2 (doubles): 3 boules each. Play 3v3 (triples): 2 boules each.
In this league we play in teams of 2 or 3
03
Throwing
Stand inside a circle drawn on the ground. Throw underarm with feet together. You can either "point" (roll your boule close to the jack) or "shoot" (knock away an opponent's boule).
04
Scoring
Only one team scores per round — the team with the closest boule. They score one point for each of their boules that is closer to the jack than the opponent's nearest boule.
First to 13 points wins the game
05
Order of Play
The team that threw the cochonnet throws first. After each throw, the team that does NOT have the nearest boule throws next. This continues until one team has no boules left.
06
The Cochonnet
Must be thrown 6–10m from the throwing circle, at least 1m from any boundary. If the jack is knocked out of bounds, the round is void and the team that did NOT throw the jack scores nothing — they replay.
Keep the jack in bounds!
